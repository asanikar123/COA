Support Our Mission

You make it possible for silenced writers to speak again.

City of Asylum is the only exiled writer sanctuary in the world not funded by governments or universities. Every dollar comes from people like you who believe creative freedom is a human right.

Your Impact Is Direct & Measurable

Currently there are 6 writers and families living on Sampsonia Way. Almost 10% of the exiled writers in the entire 80-city International Cities of Refuge Network are in Pittsburgh — because of you.

  • $50
    One Week of Groceries Provides food for a writer-in-residence and their family for one week.
  • $250
    One Month of Legal Support Funds immigration and legal counsel to help a writer and their family navigate asylum processes.
  • $500
    One Month of Medical Benefits Covers healthcare for a writer during their residency — many arrive with trauma-related health needs.
  • $1,000
    One Month of Housing Provides a safe home on Sampsonia Way for a persecuted writer and their family.
