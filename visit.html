Sampsonia Way & The Northside

Where Art Meets Safe Haven

The neighborhood around Alphabet City is home to City of Asylum's writer residences on Sampsonia Way. Houses like the iconic "House Poem" — painted with Chinese calligraphy by our first writer Huang Xiang — and the Burma House mural have transformed the street into a living gallery of freedom and resilience.

Today, 6 writers and their families live on this block. Walking down Sampsonia Way, you'll see poetry on walls, murals from around the world, and the physical evidence of what it looks like when a neighborhood decides to stand up for creative freedom.
