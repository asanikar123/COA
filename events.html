What We Host

Programs That Connect Communities

Our programming bridges cultures through four creative pillars — each one free, open to the public, and grounded in the belief that art builds understanding.

📖

Literary Readings

Acclaimed and emerging writers in all genres, with a special focus on authors in translation. 4 Nobel Laureates in Literature, 12 Pulitzer Prize Winners, 8 National Book Awardees, 11 MacArthur Fellows, and 4 U.S. Poet Laureates.

🎵

Music & Performance

Thursday Night Jazz by Pittsburgh's finest musicians debuting new work, national and international jazz groups on tour, world music, and a bit of classical/new music.

🎬

Home for Others

At no cost we provide Alphabet City to arts organizations who share our mission and have no facility of their own. A potpourri of films, poetry, jazz, and surprises.

🎉

Unique to City of Asylum

Multi-lingual Jazz-Poetry collaborations, Allegheny County Poet Laureate & Youth Poet Laureate programs, Festivals, ASL Poetry and more.

Signature Festivals

Annual Celebrations of Creative Freedom

Our flagship festivals bring together writers, musicians, filmmakers, and audiences from across the globe for multi-day celebrations.

Jazz-Poetry Concert Image
Flagship

City of Asylum Jazz Poetry Concert

Annual · Spring

Our signature event merges the spontaneity of jazz with the depth of poetry. Exiled writers read their work accompanied by world-class musicians, creating a one-of-a-kind experience that celebrates the universal language of artistic expression.

  • Live jazz-poetry collaborations on the Alphabet City stage
  • Featured readings by City of Asylum writers-in-residence
  • Open to the public, completely free of charge
Community

Alphabet City Cultural Fest

Annual · Summer

Our Northside neighborhood comes alive with an outdoor celebration of the cultures represented by our writers-in-residence. Enjoy food, music, art installations, and performances from traditions spanning five continents.

  • International food from our Cucina Alfabeto kitchen
  • Outdoor stage with global music performances
  • Children's workshops, art-making, and community mural projects

Our Stage

Alphabet City — Pittsburgh's Living Room for the World

Alphabet City was designed to feel welcoming, intimate, and alive — a place where art meets community every single week. There is nothing else like it.

Performance Stage

Intimate 150-seat venue with world-class acoustics for readings, concerts, and performances.

City of Asylum Bookstore

A bookstore unlike any other, specializing in translations. You can also shop online and order almost any book in print.

Cucina Alfabeto

Enjoy Italian-inspired dishes in our on-site restaurant before, during, or after events.

Gallery Spaces

Alphabet City spaces are available for business and private functions from 35–100.
